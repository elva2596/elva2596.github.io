Memory, 2012

bluestones, railway sleepers

two bluestones, sizes: 50×210×83cm, 49×210×83cm; two railway sleepers, each 22×16×109cm

When something comes into being, it occupies a space in this world. This is true and therefore real, and yet all these things will also vanish, returning the place in the world they emerged from. All that’s left behind is an impression, a trace. When these images and traces fade away, then all that’s left is a memory. The impression of my late teacher (Dong Zengshan, 1968-2009) has been imprinted (in relief) into these blocks of bluestone, and his soul and my memories are stored in the hollowed out spaces.
