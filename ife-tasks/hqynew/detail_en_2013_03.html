Edition of 8, 2013

marble (replicas of a single rock, cut out of several marble blocks)

8 pieces, each 55×33×32cm

These eight pieces of stone that look exactly the same are modeled on a stone that was just picked up. We can call them clones, because not only do they have the same appearance as that stone, their texture, weight and chemical composition are all essentially the same. They are copies and knock-offs of the original, but they are also the real thing itself. Among sculptures, it is usually customary to view the edition of eight copies as an original. Here the entire work of the edition of eight is taken as a single artwork. It is more about the copied batch, knock-offs and the original with imitation and other realities of society.
