The Portrait of Myself—My Breath

marble

22×35×25cm

One Breath is a series of portraits that are not actual likenesses of the people portrayed. Instead, it makes it possible to see the amount of breath each person can hold. The artist asks the people he is depicting to exhale once into a plastic bag. He then translates the bag into a marble sculpture.
