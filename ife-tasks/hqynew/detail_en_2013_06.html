Souvenir, 2013

marble (replica of a rubble piece from a ruined hutong, cut out of a single marble block)

6 pieces, different sizes from approx. 16×52×22cm to 60×60×40cm

Fragments picked up from a demolition site were carved so that they have the texture of marble. But it's not really intended that they tell the story of social issues such as relocation and destruction. So it seems fitting here to look for the direction of the artwork's meaning from the sculpture's form. Souvenir is not an artwork that expresses a social metaphor or concept, rather it is about the sculpture as a souvenir of the real situation and condition; so it is about the sculpture itself.
